OncoHealth OneUM — Clinical Prior-Authorization Criteria (v2026.2)
NOT A PATIENT RECORD — internal clinical policy. Do not use as a member document.

Lung (C34.x):
  First-line therapy for metastatic non-small cell lung cancer with high PD-L1 expression; benign tumor and in-situ carcinoma criteria follow below.
  Approve pembrolizumab monotherapy for C34.90 when PD-L1 TPS >= 50% (1L).
  Approve pembrolizumab + carboplatin + paclitaxel for C34.90 regardless of PD-L1 (1L).

Breast (C50.x):
  Approve trastuzumab for C50.911 when HER2 IHC 3+ or ISH-amplified.

Colorectal (C18.x / C20):
  Approve bevacizumab for C18.9 and C20 in combination with FOLFOX.

References: NCCN Guidelines. Codes listed are policy criteria, not diagnoses of any member.
